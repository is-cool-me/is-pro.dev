Set Up a Custom Email Address Using Your Subdomain

Create professional email addresses like hello@yourname.is-pro.dev using email forwarding services, configure MX and SPF records, and manage everything from your dashboard.

Overview

A custom email address on your is-pro.dev subdomain looks professional and builds trust. This guide covers setting up email forwarding with Cloudflare Email Routing (free) and configuring the DNS records for deliverability.

Prerequisites

  • An is-pro.dev subdomain registered at dash.is-pro.dev
  • Cloudflare proxy enabled (orange cloud) on the subdomain
  • A personal email address to receive forwarded messages

Step 1: Enable Cloudflare Email Routing

In the Cloudflare dashboard, go to Email → Email Routing. Click "Get started". Cloudflare automatically scans your zone's DNS and adds the required MX records. Email Routing is free and supports custom addresses like hello@yourname.is-pro.dev with forwarding to any destination inbox.

Step 2: Create a Custom Email Address

Under Email → Email Routing → Routes, click "Create address". Enter the local part (e.g. hello, contact, support) and the destination email where messages should arrive. You can create up to 100 free forwarding addresses on the Cloudflare free plan.

Step 3: Add SPF and DKIM Records

To avoid emails being flagged as spam, add an SPF TXT record:
v=spf1 include:_spf.mx.cloudflare.net ~all
Cloudflare Email Routing also provides a DKIM key — in Email → Settings, copy the DKIM TXT record value and add it to your DNS. DMARC is optional but recommended: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourname.is-pro.dev.

Step 4: Test Email Delivery

Send a test email from an external account (Gmail, Outlook) to your new address. Verify it arrives at your forwarding destination. Check the full email headers for SPF (spf=pass) and DKIM (dkim=pass) authentication results. Use a tool like mail-tester.com to check your domain's email reputation score.

Best Practices

  • Create separate addresses for different purposes — billing@, support@, abuse@
  • Enable catch-all forwarding temporarily during migration, then disable it to reduce spam
  • Monitor the DMARC aggregate reports (sent to the rua address) for unauthorized usage
  • Do not use the subdomain's email for critical account verification — forwarding can introduce delay

FAQ

Can I send email from my is-pro.dev subdomain?

Cloudflare Email Routing is receive-only. To send email, use a third-party SMTP service like SendGrid (free tier: 100 emails/day) configured with your subdomain as the sender.

Does email forwarding affect my subdomain's performance?

No — email routing is separate from HTTP traffic. Your website or API on the same subdomain is unaffected.
