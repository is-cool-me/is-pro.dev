GitHub Pages vs Vercel vs Netlify: Which is Best for Your Project?

Side-by-side comparison of the three most popular free static hosting platforms — covering deployment experience, custom domains, SSL, CI/CD, and when to choose each.

Overview

Side-by-side comparison of the three most popular free static hosting platforms — covering deployment experience, custom domains, SSL, CI/CD, and when to choose each.

Key Differences

Both platforms offer free hosting tiers with generous limits, but they take different approaches to deployment pipelines, edge computing, and developer experience.

Feature Comparison

Feature Option 1 Option 2
Free Tier ✅ Generous ✅ Generous
Custom Domain ✅ Included ✅ Included
SSL Certificate ✅ Auto-provisioned ✅ Auto-provisioned
CI/CD Built-in ✅ Yes ✅ Yes
Edge Functions ⚠️ Limited ✅ Full

When to Choose Each

Choose Option 1 if you need simplicity, fast setup, and basic static site hosting without edge computing requirements.

Choose Option 2 if you need edge functions, better global performance, or more advanced serverless capabilities.

Conclusion

Both platforms are excellent choices for hosting projects on is-pro.dev subdomains. The right choice depends on your specific needs — for most static sites and simple SPAs, either platform works well.
