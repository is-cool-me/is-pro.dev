Why Subdomain Requests Get Rejected (and How to Avoid It)

Common rejection patterns and a practical checklist before you register.

Published: May 2026

Most common rejection reasons

Rejected registrations usually fail on naming quality, policy risk, or obvious impersonation. Names that mimic known brands, payment services, or login providers are high-risk and are often blocked quickly.

What to check before submitting

Use a project-specific name, avoid trademarked wording, and make sure your target content is live or almost live. Empty placeholders with suspicious names create avoidable review delays.

Fast self-review checklist

Confirm you control the target service, prepare contact information on the project, and avoid misleading copy. A clean first submission gets approved faster and reduces moderation load.
