Post-Incident Review Template for DNS Outages

How to write useful incident reviews after DNS or config failures.

Published: May 2026

What a good PIR includes

A useful review documents impact, timeline, root cause, and concrete follow-up actions with owners and due dates.

Avoiding blame narratives

Focus on system gaps instead of individual mistakes. Better runbooks, guardrails, and peer review prevent repeat incidents.

Turning lessons into controls

Convert action items into checklists, monitoring alerts, and migration templates so improvements are measurable and durable.
