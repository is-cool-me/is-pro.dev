Netlify Custom Domain Setup with Free Subdomain

Connect your is-pro.dev subdomain to a Netlify site, configure DNS records for both root and www variants, and set up automatic HTTPS provisioning through Netlify Lets Encrypt.

## Introduction Netlify is a popular platform for hosting static sites, offering a free plan that includes a custom domain setup. As a developer setting up a project on a subdomain like myproject.is-pro.dev, you can take advantage of Netlify's features to create a professional-looking site with a custom domain. In this guide, we'll walk through the process of setting up a custom domain on Netlify with a free subdomain, covering the prerequisites, step-by-step instructions, common pitfalls, and best practices. ## Prerequisites Before you start, make sure you have the following: - A Netlify account: Create one if you haven't already. - A site set up on Netlify: You can create a new site or use an existing one. - A subdomain on is-pro.dev: For example, myproject.is-pro.dev. - Access to your DNS settings: You'll need to update your DNS records to point to Netlify. ## Setting Up Your Custom Domain To set up your custom domain on Netlify, follow these steps: 1. Log in to your Netlify account and go to your site's settings. 2. Click on "Domain management" and then "Add a custom domain". 3. Enter your custom domain, for example, myproject.com, and click "Verify". 4. Netlify will guide you through the verification process, which involves adding a TXT record to your DNS settings. 5. Once verified, you'll be prompted to set up your DNS records to point to Netlify. You'll need to add the following records: - CNAME record: Pointing to `myproject.netlify.app` - A record: Pointing to `75.2.70.113` (or the IP address provided by Netlify) 6. Save your changes and wait for the DNS records to propagate. ## Configuring HTTPS To enable HTTPS on your custom domain, follow these steps: 1. In your Netlify site settings, go to "Domain management" and click on "HTTPS". 2. Click on "Generate SSL certificate" and follow the prompts to verify your domain. 3. Once the SSL certificate is generated, Netlify will automatically configure HTTPS for your custom domain. 4. You can test your HTTPS setup by visiting your site in a web browser and checking for the padlock icon in the address bar. ## Common Pitfalls When setting up your custom domain on Netlify, watch out for the following common pitfalls: - Incorrect DNS settings: Double-check your DNS records to ensure they're pointing to the correct IP address or CNAME. - Propagation delays: DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate globally, so be patient if your site isn't available immediately. - SSL certificate issues: If you encounter issues with your SSL certificate, try regenerating it or contacting Netlify support. ## Best Practices To get the most out of your custom domain setup on Netlify, follow these best practices: - Use a consistent naming convention for your subdomains and custom domains. - Keep your DNS settings up to date and organized. - Test your site regularly to ensure it's available and functioning as expected. - Take advantage of Netlify's built-in features, such as caching and content delivery networks (CDNs), to improve your site's performance. ## Conclusion Setting up a custom domain on Netlify with a free subdomain like myproject.is-pro.dev is a straightforward process that can enhance your site's professionalism and credibility. By following the steps outlined in this guide, you can create a custom domain setup that's secure, reliable, and optimized for performance. Remember to stay vigilant and monitor your site's performance, and don't hesitate to reach out to Netlify support if you encounter any issues. ## FAQ Q: Can I use a custom domain with a free Netlify plan? A: Yes, Netlify offers custom domain support on its free plan, making it an excellent choice for developers on a budget. Q: How long does it take for DNS changes to propagate? A: DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate globally, but it's usually much faster, typically within a few hours. Q: Do I need to purchase an SSL certificate for my custom domain? A: No, Netlify provides free SSL certificates for custom domains, so you don't need to purchase one separately. Q: Can I use a custom domain with a subdomain on is-pro.dev? A: Yes, you can use a custom domain with a subdomain on is-pro.dev, such as myproject.is-pro.dev, and still take advantage of Netlify's custom domain features.
